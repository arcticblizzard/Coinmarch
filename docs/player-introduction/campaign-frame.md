      <h1>Coinmarch Campaign</h1>
      <p><em>A high-stakes, morally ambiguous campaign of intrigue, theft, and exploration set in the rich cityscape of Kolnmark.</em></p>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>The Pitch</h2>
          <p>In the bustling port city of Kolnmark, where fortunes are made and lost overnight, a shadowy conspiracy known as The Consortium plots to control trade and power through deception and sabotage. You are thieves, brigands, and scoundrels—unlikely heroes caught in schemes far greater than yourselves. Will you rise as kings of the underworld or become another forgotten casualty?</p>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Tone & Feel</h2>
          <ul>
              <li>Gritty</li>
              <li>Intrigue-filled</li>
              <li>Adventurous</li>
          </ul>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Themes</h2>
          <ul>
              <li>Betrayal and Trust</li>
              <li>Ambition and Consequence</li>
              <li>Secrets and Revelations</li>
          </ul>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Touchstones</h2>
          <ul>
              <li><em>The Lies of Locke Lamora</em></li>
              <li><em>Thief</em> (Video Game)</li>
              <li><em>Dishonored</em> Series</li>
          </ul>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Overview</h2>
          <p>Kolnmark, once thriving under the rule of great merchant houses, now teeters on chaos. Secret societies clash in the shadows, while relics and ruins from ancient civilizations whisper dark truths. Players navigate factions, unravel conspiracies, and shape the destiny of the city.</p>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Communities</h2>
          <p>Kolnmark is divided into districts like Bannercrest, seat of power; Crafthallow, home of artisans; and darker, criminal hubs like Bargebreaker Island.</p>
          <strong>Player Questions:</strong>
          <ul>
              <li>Which district do you call home, and what makes you loyal to it?</li>
              <li>What’s your reputation in this community?</li>
          </ul>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Ancestries</h2>
          <ul>
              <li>Human</li>
              <li>Elf</li>
              <li>Dwarf</li>
              <li>Halfling</li>
          </ul>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Classes</h2>
          <ul>
              <li>Thief</li>
              <li>Brigand</li>
              <li>Blackguard</li>
              <li>Druid (limited magic)</li>
              <li>Commander</li>
          </ul>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Player Principles</h2>
          <ul>
              <li><strong>Proactive Play:</strong> Your actions shape the narrative; take initiative.</li>
              <li><strong>Complex Morality:</strong> Heroes and villains blur in shades of grey.</li>
          </ul>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>GM Principles</h2>
          <ul>
              <li><strong>Reactive World:</strong> Let the world respond naturally to player actions.</li>
              <li><strong>Rich Detail:</strong> Flesh out the city’s factions and secrets.</li>
          </ul>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Distinctions</h2>
          <ul>
              <li>The Middens (underground ruins) provide both opportunity and peril.</li>
              <li>Magic is limited and feared—rarely trusted.</li>
          </ul>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Inciting Incident</h2>
          <p>Players are summoned by a mysterious figure named Silverhand but are ambushed by rival thieves. This betrayal opens the door to the wider conspiracy involving The Consortium.</p>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Campaign Mechanics</h2>
          <ul>
              <li>Luck mechanic with limited regeneration (1d4 per session).</li>
              <li>Skill challenges adapted from Warlock! rules.</li>
          </ul>
      </div>
  
      <div class="section">
          <h2>Session Zero Questions</h2>
          <ul>
              <li>What is your greatest ambition in Kolnmark?</li>
              <li>Describe your relationship to the underworld?</li>
              <li>What secret are you desperately trying to hide?</li>
          </ul>
