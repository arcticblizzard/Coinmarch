Campaign Frame

Coinmarch Campaign Frame

Coinmarch Campaign

A high-stakes, morally ambiguous campaign of intrigue, theft, and exploration set in the rich cityscape of Kolnmark.

The Pitch

In the bustling port city of Kolnmark, where fortunes are made and lost overnight, a shadowy conspiracy known as The Consortium plots to control trade and power through deception and sabotage. You are thieves, brigands, and scoundrels—unlikely heroes caught in schemes far greater than yourselves. Will you rise as kings of the underworld or become another forgotten casualty?

Tone & Feel

  • Gritty
  • Intrigue-filled
  • Adventurous

Themes

  • Betrayal and Trust
  • Ambition and Consequence
  • Secrets and Revelations

Touchstones

  • The Lies of Locke Lamora
  • Thief (Video Game)
  • Dishonored Series

Overview

Kolnmark, once thriving under the rule of great merchant houses, now teeters on chaos. Secret societies clash in the shadows, while relics and ruins from ancient civilizations whisper dark truths. Players navigate factions, unravel conspiracies, and shape the destiny of the city.

Communities

Kolnmark is divided into districts like Bannercrest, seat of power; Crafthallow, home of artisans; and darker, criminal hubs like Bargebreaker Island.

Player Questions:
  • Which district do you call home, and what makes you loyal to it?
  • What’s your reputation in this community?

Ancestries

  • Human
  • Elf
  • Dwarf
  • Halfling

Classes

  • Thief
  • Brigand
  • Blackguard
  • Druid (limited magic)
  • Commander

Player Principles

  • Proactive Play: Your actions shape the narrative; take initiative.
  • Complex Morality: Heroes and villains blur in shades of grey.

GM Principles

  • Reactive World: Let the world respond naturally to player actions.
  • Rich Detail: Flesh out the city’s factions and secrets.

Distinctions

  • The Middens (underground ruins) provide both opportunity and peril.
  • Magic is limited and feared—rarely trusted.

Inciting Incident

Players are summoned by a mysterious figure named Silverhand but are ambushed by rival thieves. This betrayal opens the door to the wider conspiracy involving The Consortium.

Campaign Mechanics

  • Luck mechanic with limited regeneration (1d4 per session).
  • Skill challenges adapted from Warlock! rules.

Session Zero Questions

  • What is your greatest ambition in Kolnmark?
  • Describe your relationship to the underworld?
  • What secret are you desperately trying to hide?
